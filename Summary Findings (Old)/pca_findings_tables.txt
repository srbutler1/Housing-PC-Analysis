Key Findings:

1. Component Evolution:
   - PC1 shifted from pure economic factors to include price/cost considerations
   - PC2 maintained focus on financial conditions but adapted during crises
   - PC3 consistently captured market dynamics and price changes

2. Crisis Impact:
   - 2008-2011 showed unique dispersion of variance across components
   - Pandemic period demonstrated high concentration in first two components
   - Both crises elevated the importance of income factors

3. Recent Trends:
   - Construction prices gained prominence in factor loadings
   - Supply-side factors became more influential
   - More complex interplay between traditional economic and market-specific factors

4. Structural Changes:
   - Evolution from simple economic fundamentals to complex factor interactions
   - Increased importance of supply-side constraints
   - Growing influence of construction costs and housing prices
   - More prominent role of income during crisis periods
